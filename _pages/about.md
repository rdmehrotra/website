Hi, I’m Rohan Mehrotra, an Electrical Engineering student at Georgia Tech with interests in signal processing, robotics, and embedded systems.

I enjoy working at the intersection of hardware and software, building systems that combine technical design with real-world applications. My experience includes programming in C and Python, working with microcontrollers, and developing projects that integrate both hardware and software.

Beyond academics, I am involved in leadership and community activities, including student organizations and campus initiatives. I value collaboration, problem-solving, and continuous learning, and I am always looking for opportunities to grow both technically and professionally.

Outside of engineering, I enjoy cooking, baking, music, and staying active.
